Contact

For most enquiries, please contact the Parish Clerk. The Clerk is the council's Proper Officer and will direct your query to the right place.

Response times: The Clerk aims to respond to all enquiries within 5 working days. For urgent matters please indicate this in your message.

Reporting accessibility issues

If you have difficulty using this website or accessing any documents, please contact the Parish Clerk using the details above. Please include:

  • The web address (URL) of the page you were visiting
  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Your name and contact details (so we can respond)

We aim to respond to accessibility complaints within 14 days. For more information, see our Accessibility Statement.

Freedom of Information requests

To make a Freedom of Information request, please email or write to the Parish Clerk with your request. We aim to respond within 20 working days. For a list of information we routinely publish, see our Publication Scheme.

Complaints

If you have a complaint about the council's services or administration, please see our Complaints Procedure. Complaints about the conduct of a councillor are handled by the Monitoring Officer at Westmorland and Furness Council.

Data protection: Any personal data you provide when contacting the council will be processed in accordance with our Privacy Notice.
