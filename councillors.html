Register of Members' Interests

All councillors are required by the Localism Act 2011 to register their disclosable pecuniary interests and other registerable interests. Declarations are held by the Monitoring Officer at Westmorland and Furness Council and are also published by the parish council below.

If you wish to inspect the register, please contact the Parish Clerk.

Data protection: Councillors' personal contact details are published in line with the requirements of the Localism Act 2011 and the Local Government Transparency Code 2015. For more information, see our Privacy Notice.

Co-option and vacancies

If a casual vacancy arises on the council, it may be filled by co-option. Any vacancy will be advertised on this website and on the council noticeboard. If you are interested in becoming a parish councillor, please contact the Clerk for more information.
